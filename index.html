DISCOVER THE GAME

Athletic Games is a next-generation track and field mobile experience that redefines the genre. Step into the world of competitive athletics, customize your athletes, and compete in thrilling championship events.

Featuring unique events such as the 4x100m relay, 4x200m relay, 400m hurdles, and even mixed relays, the game offers both variety and realism. Build and train your athletes, where every stat and upgrade directly impacts performance — adding strategy to every race.

What sets Athletic Games apart is the ability to roleplay with your athletes. Even with similar stats, no two competitors feel the same — their strengths, playstyle, and your choices make each one unique.

CREATE YOUR OWN ATHLETES

Bring your champions to life with full customization. Even when two athletes share similar stats, your choices in training, strategy, and style make each one feel truly unique — letting you roleplay and build a team that reflects your vision.

COMPETE IN TOURNAMENTS

Tournament Mode is here, delivering one of the most exciting experiences in Athletic Games. Build your dream team of athletes and battle through full-scale championships to earn medals, trophies, and huge coin rewards.

Every tournament mirrors real-world competition — from Heats to Semi-Finals to Finals — bringing the intensity of championship athletics straight to your screen.

Depth, strategy, and replayability define Tournament Mode. Craft balanced teams, outsmart your rivals, and chase the thrill of guiding your roster all the way to the gold.

MULTIPLAYER

Athletic Games brings the competition to life with both LAN and online multiplayer modes.

Challenge friends over LAN for fast, local races on the same network, or take the competition online to face opponents from around the world in random matchups or private rooms with friends.

The online mode is back and better than ever — fully live and ready for real-time global races. Put your athletes to the test against the best players worldwide!
