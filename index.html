Athletic Games

Athletic Games is a track and field-themed mobile game that brings a fresh approach to the genre. Players can compete in a wide variety of events, customize athletes, and experience an immersive simulation of championship competitions.

With unique events like the 4x00 metres, 4x200 metres, 400-metre hurdles, and mixed relays, Athletic Games lets fans of the sport enjoy realistic gameplay and progression. Character creation allows players to develop athletes whose stats impact their performance, adding a layer of strategy and personalization.

One of the most enjoyable aspects of the game is the ability to roleplay with your athletes. Even with similar stats, each athlete can feel unique based on how you choose to use and build them.

Events Include:

100 metres, 200 metres, 400 metres, 800 metres, 1500 metres, 60 metres, 100 and 110 metres hurdles, 400 metres hurdles, 4x100 metre relay, 4x200 metre relay, 4x400 metre relay, 2x2x400 metre relay, long jump, triple jump, javelin throw — and more coming soon.

Tournament Mode

The Tournament Mode is now fully released and offers one of the most exciting and polished experiences in Athletic Games. Players can bring together their customized athletes and compete in full-scale championships, earning medals, trophies, and large coin rewards.

Tournaments are structured with realistic rounds: Heats, Semi-Finals, and Finals — offering an immersive championship feel that mirrors real-world athletic meets.

Tournament mode adds incredible depth and replayability to the game, allowing players to strategize, build balanced teams, and experience the competitive thrill of taking home gold with their own roster of athletes.


Multiplayer

Athletic Games features both LAN multiplayer and online multiplayer.

LAN multiplayer allows players on the same local network to race each other. Online multiplayer lets users compete globally — whether in random matchups or custom/private rooms with friends.

The online feature has been reintroduced and is now live again. Compete in real-time with players worldwide and test your athletes against the best!
